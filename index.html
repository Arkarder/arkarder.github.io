Uptime Calendar

  • Navigate pleasantly through weeks

    Uptime lets you easily and pleasantly navigate from week-to-week and from day-to-day.

  • Create reusable events

    Uptime lets you create events that can be reused, and immediately schedule all the days that apply.

  • Schedule multiple events, for multiple days

    Uptime lets you very quickly schedule multiple days worth of events in a matter of seconds, without having to be a pro.

  • Sync with your other calendars

    Uptime can sync all the events you create with one of your calendars (iCloud, Google, Microsoft Exchange, etc.) so they can be viewed from your other devices.
