Uptime Calendar

Simply the best calendar for recurring things

  • Navigate pleasantly through time

    Uptime lets you fluidly and pleasantly navigate through days, weeks and months.

  • Create reusable events

    Uptime lets you create events that can be reused, and schedule multiple occurrences upfront.

  • Schedule events for multiple days

    Uptime lets you schedule one or more weeks worth of events in a matter of seconds, and still be relaxed.

  • Sync with your other calendars

    Uptime can sync all the events you create with one of your calendars (iCloud, Google, Microsoft Exchange, etc.) so they can be viewed from your other devices.
